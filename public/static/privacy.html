Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At LAKSHYA 2K26, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by LAKSHYA 2K26 and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information required for event registration and verification purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Identity: Full Name, College Roll Number.
  • Contact Information: Email Address, Mobile Number.
  • Academic Details: College Name, Department, Year of Study.
  • Payment Information: Transaction IDs and Payment Status (Note: We do not store Credit/Debit card numbers or CVVs. These are handled securely by Razorpay).

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To register you for specific events and competitions.
  • To generate digital certificates and ID cards.
  • To communicate with you regarding event schedules, updates, and changes.
  • To prevent fraud and maintain the security of our platform.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates.

Payment Processing: We use Razorpay for processing payments. Razorpay does not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.

4. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Our database uses strong encryption for sensitive data like passwords.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@testify-lac.com.
