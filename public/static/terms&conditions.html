Welcome to LAKSHYA 2K26. By registering for events and making payments on our platform, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.

1. Registration Policy

  • Participation is open to all college students with a valid ID card.
  • Students must carry their registration receipt (digital or print) and college ID on the day of the event.
  • Team events require the details of all team members during registration. The team leader is responsible for the accuracy of this data.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to cancel or reschedule events due to unforeseen circumstances.

2. Payment Terms

All online payments for event registration are processed securely through Razorpay, our authorized payment gateway partner.

  • We accept payments via UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets.
  • No Card Data Storage: LAKSHYA 2K26 does not store your card details. All transaction data is encrypted and handled securely by Razorpay in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
  • Convenience fees or platform fees (if applicable) charged by the payment gateway are non-refundable.
  • Once a payment is successful, a confirmation email and receipt will be sent to your registered email address.

3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Given the nature of the event planning and logistics, the following refund rules apply:

  • Strictly Non-Refundable: Registration fees once paid are non-refundable and non-transferable, except in the specific case mentioned below.
  • Event Cancellation: If an event is cancelled by the LAKSHYA 2K26 committee and not rescheduled, a full refund for that specific event fee will be processed within 7-10 working days.
  • Duplicate Payments: If you have been charged twice for the same transaction due to a technical error, the extra amount will be automatically refunded by the gateway within 5-7 business days.

4. Transaction Failures

In case of a transaction failure where money is deducted from your account but the registration status shows "Pending":

  • Please wait for 15-30 minutes for the status to update.
  • If the status remains "Pending," the amount is usually auto-refunded by your bank within 3-5 working days.
  • You may contact us at support@testify-lac.com with your transaction ID for assistance.

5. Code of Conduct

All participants are expected to maintain discipline and decorum during the fest. Any form of misconduct, harassment, or violation of campus rules will lead to immediate disqualification and removal from the venue without refund.
