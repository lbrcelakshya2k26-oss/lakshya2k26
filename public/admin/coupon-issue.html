How this works

Use this tool to issue standard food coupons to anyone.

  • Coupons are not restricted to any specific stall.
  • Each email will receive 2 Food Coupons by default.
  • Recipients will get an automated email with a secure link to view their coupons.
  • If a user already has coupons, the system will just resend their link.
